Who can be a member in an IVCS?

Membership in an IVCS is opened to individuals above the age of 18 years and lives in the village(s) covered by the IVCS.

Membership in an IVCS will also be open to SHGs/ Members of SHGs, Producer Groups/Members of PGs which operate/who lives in the village(s) covered by IVCS.

Minimum of 15 adults can start, as promoters, the process of forming an IVCS. Of the 15 promoters, efforts should be made that at least 05 will be women.

While mobilizing membership, minimum 50% of them should be women.

Wherever a Producer Group (PG) is formed, the PG and Members of the PG are required to become Members of the IVCS also by subscribing to the Shares of the IVCS.

Key message for the Members:

The IVCS is an institution promoted by the people and is not a government institution.

The participation of all residents in the village(s) covered by the IVCS is most important as it is this which will make the IVCS strong.

It should be the endeavour of the promoters and members of the Managing Committees of the IVCS to make all adults of all households in the village(s) to become members of the IVCS.

Members have a place in their own village to save their hard earned income no matter what amount and earn interest income also. Members can also get loans at reasonable rates of interest whenever they need especially in times of emergencies and for expanding their economic and business activities.

It would be difficult for the IVCS to develop through thrift and lending activities/ services. The IVCS would also need to identify and take up other economic activities also to become viable and earn good income and be able to pay dividends to their members.
